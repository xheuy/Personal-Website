「 DOCUMENT RESEARCH 」

Perception of music

Music is a kind of organized sound composed of many elements. The brain processes music in almost all areas of the brain and the nervous system. The brain uses feature extraction and functional integration to process music: the brain restores a piece of music signal to its most basic element, and then presents these basic signals to different regions of the brain, processed in parallel by different nervous systems without interfering with each other. This is A bottom-up process.

Researchers from Aalto University conducted a survey themed “Emotional Effects on Physiological Changes”. 700 volunteers from Finland, Sweden and Taiwan took part in the study. And the researchers examined temperatures in different parts of their bodies under different emotions and used the results to build this thermal imaging digram.
