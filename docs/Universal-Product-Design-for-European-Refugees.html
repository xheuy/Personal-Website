「 INCREASING REFUGEES 」

According to the data collected from UNHCR (United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees) in 2015, there are 595,000,00 people destitute and homeless all around the word, the number is nearly doubled when it’s compared to the number ten years ago. And 195,000,00 of them are refugees.

CAUSES

Mind Map

On one hand, the problem is mainly caused by war refugees. The chaos and turbulence caused by war as well as ISIS increasingly fuel the issue.

Global Polarization

On the other hand, it was also because of polarization during globalization. For the people from some relatively backward economy, Northern and Western Europe would be the solid choice for them.

SEVERE SITUATION

In 2014, refugee number grow fastest in Europe, nearly 220,000 refugees went across Mediterranean and reached Europe. Sorry to say, 2850 people died or missed during the journey.

Turkey contains the largest number of refugees in the world.

Lebanon contains the highest proportion of the world's refugees. Over 21% of the total population.

The largest number of refugees in the world: Syria, Afghanistan and Somali.

The report also shows that more than half of the refugees are under the age of 18, of which only about half of the children have the opportunity to receive primary education, and ultimately only 1% of them could enter college.
